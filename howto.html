Setting up a meetup

Speakers

Request speakers and find a data that works for them

You can also use this form to ask people for suggestions.

We also have a spreadsheet with previous speakers and future speakers. Add the new speakers to the list.

Confirm the location

Talk to Impact Hub to reserve the location as early as possible! Basically once you've got enough speakers for a date, reserve the location!

If the Impact Hub should be booked, try Karl der Grosse, Dynamo or Quartierhaus Kreis 5.

Find out what connectors the projector has, so you can give speakers a heads up info on that!

We know:

  • Karl der Grosse: HDMI
  • Quartierhaus Kreis 5: VGA

Announce

Latest the day before the previous meetup you should put up the new meetup on meetup.com

Ask speakers for a title and a short description, add that to Meetup.com and our website, but do not select to "announce" the Meetup yet.

At the meetup you should announce the next meetup date and if possible the speakers and topics.

The day after the meetup (unless it's a Friday, then wait until the following Monday), you announce the meetup on Slack and Meetup.com

Hosting the meetup

Be at the venue early

Be at the venue at least 30 minutes in advance!

Check the venue

Make sure the projector works, there's enough chairs and the setup makes sense (e.g. everybody can see the screen well enough)

Also make sure the atmosphere is nice. Clean up or open the windows if necessary.

Signage

Bring along printed signs and a marker pen, so you can add arrows.

Print at least three of them and put them up at the venue to guide people to the right room or area.

After the meetup

Collect slides

Ask the speakers to give you their slides, either as links or on a USB stick or something.

It's a great idea to publish slides online, put them on the website.

Check the venue

Make sure the projector works, there's enough chairs and the setup makes sense (e.g. everybody can see the screen well enough)

Also make sure the atmosphere is nice. Clean up or open the windows if necessary.

Clean up

Put back chairs and tables as they were before the meetup

Remove bottles, glasses, etc. and make sure the venue is clean.

Lock the venue and bring back the key, if you have one.
